# Research-Only Compliance

Eurogas Nexus V1 must preserve a clear research-only boundary.

## Prohibited Product Behavior

- Execute trades.
- Enter orders.
- Route orders.
- Capture trades as a system of record.
- Submit nominations.
- Provide official approvals.
- Provide legal advice.
- Provide official trading recommendations.
- Auto-trade.
- Replace an ETRM.
- Integrate company SSO/OIDC in V1.

## Human Review

Future analysis outputs must make human review requirements explicit where
relevant. The platform should support research workflows, not autonomous
commercial action.

## Entitlement

Unknown commercial data entitlement must fail closed. Export should be blocked
or marked unavailable until entitlement is known and documented.

## Audit Readiness

Future audit events should capture actor, action, resource, policy decision,
source references, and timestamp.
